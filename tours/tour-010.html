Sustainable Farm Visit & Organic Lunch

Connect with Saudi Arabia's agricultural heritage

3hUp to 20 guestsRiyadh Park Mall, north parking lot

About This Experience

Experience the growing farm-to-table movement in Saudi Arabia with this enriching visit to a working sustainable farm on the outskirts of Riyadh. Meet the farmers who are pioneering water-efficient agriculture in the desert using hydroponics and vertical farming techniques. Pick your own seasonal vegetables, learn about composting and water conservation, and help prepare a communal lunch using the farm's fresh produce. The tour also covers the history of Saudi agriculture, from ancient oasis farming to modern innovations aligned with Vision 2030 food security goals.

Highlights

  • Hydroponics and vertical farming demonstrations
  • Pick-your-own seasonal vegetables experience
  • Hands-on communal lunch preparation with farm produce
  • Learn about Saudi Vision 2030 food security initiatives
